Who we are?

NASBiotech

A subsidiary of F&D Advanced Biotech Company, NASBiotech is headquartered in Berlin and remains up-to-date on the latest advancements in biotechnology, with a special focus on Real-time PCR technology. Specializing in Real-time PCR, NASBiotech strives to deliver premium quality at competitive prices, complemented by exceptional services to clients and customers across the EU, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East markets.

Manufacturer

NASBiotech excels in delivering exceptional products and solutions in the realm of analytics, prioritizing precision, safety, clarity, and knowledge, especially in the field of prevention. Specializing in molecular DNA and RNA analysis, NASBiotech demonstrates expertise in the development and application of Real-time PCR methods, including qPCR. Our product range is designed to provide cutting-edge solutions for the reliable analysis of both food and feed. Committed to meeting customer specifications and surpassing expectations, NASBiotech places a strong emphasis on product integrity, safe manufacturing and distribution, and compliance with all relevant regulations. The NASBiotech team is dedicated to offering excellent service and high-quality products at competitive prices. With a global presence spanning various countries, NASBiotech can be accessed directly under F&D Advanced Biotech or through collaborations with local distributors. This international accessibility allows us to collaboratively enhance diagnostics worldwide, regardless of your location.
